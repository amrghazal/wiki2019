Contribution

The iGEM Lund 2019 teams contribution to the registry was to the part BBa_J04450,  Red Fluorescent Protein (RFP). RFP was incorporated in the construct to compare the protein expression in different conditions.


Three different E. coli strains were tested in three different complex media.

The strains that were tested: 

  • E. coli TG1
  • E. coli BL21 (DE3)
  • E. coli Nissle 1917 (EcN)

While the prepared  media were:

  • LB Media 
  • TB Media 
  • Enriched Complex Media

For detailed information about RFP and our contribution, go to the Registry.
