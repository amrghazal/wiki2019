We at iGEM Lund 2019 have genetically modifying a probiotic bacteria - E. coli Nissle 1917 - to increase its ability to absorb and accumulate lead and arsenic. Lead and arsenic, among many other toxic metals are generating problems across the globe, especially in countries with poor water infrastructure such as Bangladesh and Argentina. Our product, PRODEACC, could be a natural and sustainable remedy to prevent toxic metal-poisoning by disposing them from your body and returning them to earth. The probiotic bacteria will enter the gut and GI-tract and act as a sponge, absorbing and accumulating lead, arsenic and mercury. It will then be excreted through natural pathways, thus removing the toxic metals from your body. With a proof of concept established, our method could be implemented in various bacteria acting on numerous metals or toxic substances.
