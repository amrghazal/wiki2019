We at iGEM Lund 2019 are genetically modifying a probiotic bacteria - E. coli Nissle 1917 - to increase its ability to absorb and accumulate lead, arsenic and mercury. Lead, arsenic and mercury are generating problems across the globe, especially in countries with poor water infrastructure such as Bangladesh and Argentina. Our product, Prodeacc, will be a natural and sustainable remedy to prevent toxic metal-poisoning by disposing them from your body and returning them to earth. The probiotic bacteria will enter your gut and GI-tract and act as a sponge, absorbing and accumulating lead, arsenic and mercury. It will then be excreted through natural pathways, thus removing the toxic metals from your body. With a proof of concept established, our method could be implemented in various bacteria acting on numerous metals or toxic substances.

Description

We're focusing on the three toxic metals: arsenic, lead and mercury. For each heavy metal we’ve identified the pathways of transport and accumulation in means of overexpressing some of the necessary proteins involved. We're doing so by introducing plasmids, one for each toxic metal. Read more...

Model

By modelling the bodies uptake of metals we can investigate the effectiveness of Prodeacc. We will then be able to apply our model to real-life situations of arsenic, lead and mercury-poisoning, but also to other metals and toxic substances.

Human practices

Our efforts in human practices has helped us design our project and realize what needs to be done to make Prodeacc reality.
